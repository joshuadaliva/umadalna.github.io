    Course Description

    Unlock your creative potential with "Canva For Beginners: Create Stunning Designs." This comprehensive course is designed to take you from novice to proficient designer using Canva, a powerful and user-friendly graphic design tool. Whether you're looking to create eye-catching social media posts, professional presentations, or personal projects, this course will provide you with the skills and confidence to produce stunning designs with ease.


    Duration

  • Total Duration: 10 hours
  • Weeks to Complete: Approximately 2-4 weeks, depending on your pace

    • What You'll Learn

  • Introduction to Canva: Understand the basics of Canva, its interface, and key features.
  • Design Principles: Learn essential design principles such as color theory, typography, and layout.
  • Advanced Techniques: Explore more advanced features like animations, collaboration tools, and exporting designs in various formats.

    • Prerequisites

  • Basic Knowledge of Excel: Familiarity with Excel functions and features.
  • Interest in Programming: While no prior programming experience is required, an eagerness to learn coding concepts is beneficial.
